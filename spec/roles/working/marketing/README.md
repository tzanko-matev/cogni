# Role: Marketing

## Point of view
I craft the story and materials that explain Cogni to the market. I need crisp positioning and real examples.

## I care about
- Clear narrative and differentiation.
- Consistent terminology and claims.
- Visuals and artifacts to showcase.

## I need from the spec
- Product summary and goals.
- Example outputs and report screenshots.
- Target personas and use cases.

## Success signals
- Messaging resonates with target buyers.
- Materials are easy to keep updated.
- Launches align with product reality.
